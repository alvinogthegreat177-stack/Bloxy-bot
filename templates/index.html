Help & FAQ

How do I switch AI models?

Click the model selector in the top-left of the chat. Choose from OpenAI, Groq, OpenRouter, or Kimi models. Your selection applies to the current and future chats.

How do I use tools like Web Search?

Click the Tools button in the top navigation bar, then select a tool such as Web Search, Deep Research, or Translator. An active tool badge will appear above the composer.

Can I upload files or PDFs?

Yes — click the attachment icon in the composer to upload a PDF or image. Use the PDF Chat tool for document Q&A and Image Analysis for vision tasks (requires a vision-capable model).

How do I export my chats?

Click "Export chats" in the sidebar footer. You can export in JSON or Markdown format. Use the Import button in the top nav to restore a previous export.

What is Deep Research?

Deep Research uses Tavily and Exa to search multiple sources simultaneously and synthesize a comprehensive, cited report on your topic. It takes longer than a regular search but provides much more depth.

Is my data private?

Your conversations are stored locally in your browser and on our secure servers if you're signed in. We do not sell your data. API calls are made directly to the respective AI providers.

Delete chat?

This action cannot be undone. The chat and all its messages will be permanently deleted.
